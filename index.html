Our Story

Design born from darkness

L'Amour Interior Designs emerged from a singular conviction: that truly extraordinary spaces are defined not by what is illuminated, but by what is held in shadow.

Our team combines traditional craftsmanship with AI-augmented visualisation tools, delivering a process that is both deeply human and breathtakingly precise. We take on fewer than twelve projects annually, ensuring every client receives our complete attention.

Based in India, working globally. From luxury penthouses to boutique hotels, our signature aesthetic adapts to every culture while retaining its essential character: restrained, confident, unforgettable.
