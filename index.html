A 60-minute conversation to understand your vision, lifestyle, space, and aspirations. We listen before we design.

02

We present 2–3 distinct concept directions — mood boards, spatial layouts, material palettes, and lighting schemes.

03

Full technical drawings, furniture specifications, bespoke piece commissioning, and supplier coordination.

Our Story

Design born from darkness

L'Amour Interior Designs emerged from a singular conviction: that truly extraordinary spaces are defined not by what is illuminated, but by what is held in shadow.

Our team combines traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge design tools, delivering a process that is both deeply human and breathtakingly precise. We take on fewer than twelve projects annually, ensuring every client receives our complete attention.

Based in India, working globally. From luxury penthouses to boutique hotels, our signature aesthetic adapts to every culture while retaining its essential character: restrained, confident, unforgettable.

Refund Policy

Company Information

L'Amour Interior Designs is based in India and operates as a premium digital and physical design services provider. We specialise in luxury interior design consultation and visualisation.

Digital Products & Consultations

Our design consultation services are primarily bespoke and non-refundable once work has commenced. Digital deliverables (mood boards, design documents, renders) are non-refundable once delivered.

Refund Conditions

  • Duplicate charges within 24 hours of payment
  • Technical failure on our part preventing delivery of agreed services
  • Unauthorised transactions (with proper verification and bank confirmation)
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the charge date

How to Request

Email us at hello@lamourdesigns.in with your name, order details, and reason for the request. We respond within 5 business days.
